I am a final-year PhD student at the Centre for Speech Technology Research (CSTR) at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Professor Simon King and Dr. Catherine Lai. My research examines how listeners perceive and evaluate stylistic variation in synthetic speech, with a focus on spontaneous and conversational styles. Broadly, I am also broadly interested in Explainable AI (XAI) frameworks for speech models and the development of speech technologies for low-resource languages. I plan to submit my thesis by the end of this year and am currently seeking opportunities in both academia and industry. Feel free to reach out!
